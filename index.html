FTBL+FIT

ELITE SUPPORT. EVERY STEP OF THE WAY.

FTBL+FIT is a Cardiff-based football and fitness coaching service founded by Shaun Edwards. The mission is to help footballers and everyday athletes unlock their full potential — physically, technically, mentally and tactically — through personalised coaching both online and in-person.

FTBL+FIT bridges the gap between elite support and real-life accessibility with performance-based coaching for footballers and fitness-focused individuals across all levels.
